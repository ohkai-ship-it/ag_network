# Meeting Prep: von Rundstedt

## Meeting Type: Discovery

## Agenda
1. Introduction and rapport building (5 minutes)
2. Understanding von Rundstedt's current challenges (10 minutes)
3. Exploring goals and priority initiatives (10 minutes)
4. Discussing current solutions and gaps (10 minutes)
5. Next steps and potential solutions (5 minutes)

## Discovery Questions
- Can you elaborate on the main challenges you're currently facing in your business operations?
- What are the key objectives for von Rundstedt in the upcoming year?
- How are your current solutions addressing your needs, and where do you see gaps?
- Who are the primary stakeholders involved in decision-making for new solutions?
- How does your team measure success in this area?

## Stakeholder Map
- **CEO**: Assumed to oversee company strategy and decision-making
- **COO**: Assumed to focus on operational efficiency and process improvements
- **HR Director**: Assumed to manage talent acquisition and employee development
- **IT Manager**: Assumed to evaluate and implement technology solutions

## Listen For
- Mention of budget availability for new solutions
- Expression of dissatisfaction with current providers
- Interest in new trends and technologies
- Indications of upcoming strategic initiatives
- Commitment to a follow-up meeting

## Close Plan
Suggest scheduling a follow-up meeting to discuss tailored solutions and possible demonstrations based on the challenges and goals shared by von Rundstedt.
