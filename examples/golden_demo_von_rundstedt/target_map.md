# Target Map: von Rundstedt

## Personas

### Chief Financial Officer
- **Role**: economic_buyer
- **Hypothesis**: As the CFO, they are responsible for budget approvals. Highlight cost efficiency and ROI to align with their financial objectives.
- _(Assumption)_

### Head of Human Resources
- **Role**: champion
- **Hypothesis**: This role is crucial for driving the adoption of solutions that impact workforce management. Engage them by showing how the solution aligns with HR initiatives.
- _(Assumption)_

### IT Manager
- **Role**: technical_evaluator
- **Hypothesis**: They assess technical requirements and integrations. Address any technical specifications and integrations with existing systems.
- _(Assumption)_

### Head of Change Management
- **Role**: influencer
- **Hypothesis**: They are likely to shape the organizational approach to new tools. Demonstrate how the solution supports change management processes.
- _(Assumption)_

### Senior Outplacement Consultant
- **Role**: end_user
- **Hypothesis**: Ensure that the solution enhances their daily operations and effectiveness in delivering their services.
- _(Assumption)_
