## Follow-up Sequence
1. Follow up with an email two days later to check in if they've had a chance to consider.
2. Send a LinkedIn message if no response after a week to keep the conversation open.
3. Offer a piece of valuable content related to HR trends on the third follow-up.
4. Send a final email a week later to confirm if they’re still interested in discussing further.
5. Make a soft phone call as a last step to see if there's an opportunity to connect live.
